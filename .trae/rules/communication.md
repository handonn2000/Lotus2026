# Communication & Collaboration Rules

Rules for effective communication and collaboration within the Lotus2026 team.

## Team Interactions
- **Respectful Communication**: Maintain a professional and supportive tone in all interactions.
- **Constructive Feedback**: Focus on the code and the product, not the individual, during reviews.
- **Shared Ownership**: Every team member is responsible for the overall quality of the product.

## Issue Tracking
- **Descriptive Titles**: Use clear and concise titles for all issues and pull requests.
- **Detailed Descriptions**: Provide enough context, steps to reproduce, and expected behavior in issue reports.
- **Link Issues**: Always link pull requests to the corresponding issues they resolve.

## Meetings & Syncs
- **Be Prepared**: Come to sprint planning and reviews with necessary updates and feedback.
- **Document Decisions**: Record key technical decisions and action items in the project wiki or README.
- **Asynchronous First**: Use project management tools and Slack for non-urgent communication to respect focus time.
